System Overview

The SanMo CRM & ERP System is a centralized, cloud-based business management platform designed to streamline and automate organizational operations across multiple departments. The system integrates Human Resources, Accounts & Finance, SEO Operations, AI Content Creation, and Software Development Management into a single unified environment.

The platform is designed to improve operational efficiency, increase employee productivity, centralize business data, and provide real-time insights for management decision-making. In addition to supporting internal business processes, the system is structured as a scalable Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solution that can be offered to external organizations.

Purpose of the System

The primary purpose of the system is to eliminate manual processes, reduce operational complexity, and provide a centralized workspace where departments can collaborate efficiently.

The system enables organizations to:

  • ✓ Manage employees and administrative operations
  • ✓ Track attendance, leave, payroll, and performance
  • ✓ Control financial activities and reporting
  • ✓ Manage creative and marketing workflows
  • ✓ Monitor SEO activities and campaign performance
  • ✓ Organize software development projects and tasks
  • ✓ Generate real-time reports and business insights
  • ✓ Improve accountability through workflow automation

Dashboard Module

The Dashboard Module provides role-based access to key business metrics, analytics, operational summaries, and performance indicators. Each user role is presented with a customized dashboard tailored to their responsibilities and daily activities.

Admin Dashboard

Provides a complete overview of employees, attendance, projects, payroll, invoices, expenses, vouchers, payments and organizational performance.

HR Dashboard

Focuses on employee management, attendance tracking, leave distribution, recruitment activities, interviews and approval workflows.

Finance Dashboard

Displays financial performance, expenses, payments, invoices, vouchers, cash flow summaries and transaction reports.

Employee Dashboard

Allows employees to monitor attendance, working hours, leave balance, performance metrics, notifications and assigned projects.

Government Holidays

This section is used to manage all government and public holidays. Administrators can create, update, delete, and publish official holidays for the organization. The system also allows uploading government holiday PDF documents for record keeping and future reference.

  • ✓ Create Government Holiday
  • ✓ Upload Holiday PDF
  • ✓ Store Holiday Documents in Database
  • ✓ Edit Holiday Information
  • ✓ Replace Uploaded PDF
  • ✓ Delete Holiday Records
  • ✓ Holiday Date Management
  • ✓ Holiday Calendar View
  • ✓ Download Holiday PDF
When a government holiday is created, the holiday information and uploaded PDF document are stored in the system. Administrators can later update, replace, or delete the holiday record as needed. Employees can view published holidays from their dashboard, holiday calendar, and upcoming holiday widgets.
