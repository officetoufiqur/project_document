KPI Rating Module

The KPI (Key Performance Indicator) Rating Module is used to evaluate employee performance on a monthly basis. HR Managers and Team Leaders can assign performance ratings to employees based on their work quality, productivity, attendance, task completion, and overall contribution.

1. Employee KPI Rating

HR and Team Leaders can evaluate employees every month by assigning a rating score out of 100.

  • ✓ Monthly Employee Evaluation
  • ✓ Rating Score (0 - 100)
  • ✓ Performance Remarks
  • ✓ Team Leader Assessment
  • ✓ HR Assessment

2. Performance Impact

KPI ratings directly affect employee performance statistics and performance dashboards throughout the system.

  • ✓ Performance Percentage Calculation
  • ✓ Monthly Performance Trend
  • ✓ Employee Ranking
  • ✓ Productivity Analysis
  • ✓ Performance Reports

Rating Scale

Score Performance Level
90 - 100 Excellent
80 - 89 Very Good
70 - 79 Good
60 - 69 Average
Below 60 Needs Improvement

KPI Rating Workflow

Employee Activities → Monthly Review → Team Leader Rating → HR Rating → Final KPI Score → Performance Updated
