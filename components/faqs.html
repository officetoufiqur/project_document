Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I reset my password?

Open your profile page and use the Change Password option. If you cannot access your account, contact the system administrator.

2. How do I apply for leave?

Go to Employee → Leave Section and submit a leave request. HR or Admin will review and approve/reject the request.

3. What happens after my leave is approved?

Your leave balance will be automatically deducted and attendance will be generated for the approved leave dates.

4. How can I request an attendance correction?

Open Attendance Details and submit an attendance correction request with a valid reason.

5. How can I download my attendance report?

Open Attendance Details and click the Download PDF option.

6. How do I view my assigned projects?

Assigned projects are available from your Profile page and Dashboard.

7. How can I request an AI tool?

Open your Profile → Credentials tab and submit a tool request. Your Team Leader will review the request.

8. Where can I see my assigned assets?

All assigned company assets are available in your Employee Profile under the Assets section.

9. How do I view my payslips?

Open the Payslip section to view and download your approved salary slips.

10. Why can't I access some modules?

Access to modules depends on your assigned role and permissions. Contact your administrator if additional access is required.

11. Where can I see system notifications?

Notifications are available from the Header Notification menu, Dashboard widgets, and the Notification Module.

12. Can I view another employee's information?

No. Access to employee information is controlled by system roles and permissions.

Need Additional Help?

If you cannot find the answer to your question, please contact your HR Department, Team Leader, or System Administrator for assistance.
