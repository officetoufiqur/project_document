Employee Management Module

The Employee Management Module is responsible for managing the complete employee lifecycle within the organization. This module allows administrators and HR personnel to create, update, manage, and monitor employee information, projects, tasks, leave requests, and assigned company assets.

Employee Profile

The Employee Profile page provides a complete overview of employee information, assigned projects, leave balances, uploaded documents, login credentials and assigned company assets.
